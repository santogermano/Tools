Albanian Riviera — Dhërmi
Top pick

Europe's most underrated coast — wild beaches, direct flight, genuinely off-mainstream

BeachNatureAdventureRemoteNightlife
✈ EIN → TIA direct · 2h 40min

🏨 Drymades Inn guesthouse

Budget use€380–490 / €500
EIN → TIA (Tirana) — Ryanair or Wizz Air Malta
Departs 09:20 or 12:50 · Return €80–120/pp · Book now for July
Airport → Dhërmi: rental car ~3.5h south via SH8 coast road · ~€35–45/day
Day 1
Fly EIN→TIA, drive to Dhërmi
Pick up rental car at Tirana Airport. Drive SH8 south (~3.5h). Check in to Drymades Inn. Beach dinner at a local taverna.
Day 2
Drymades Beach day
White pebble beach, turquoise water, pine hills backdrop. Afternoon: hike to Gjipe Canyon (1.5h, no car access). Evening: beach bar DJ sets.
Day 3
Himara old town + Palasa Beach
20 min north. Hilltop village above the coast. Swim at Livadhi Beach. Afternoon: Pirate's Cave boat trip.
Day 4
Drive south to Sarandë
~1.5h drive. Butrint National Park (UNESCO ruins, €10/pp). Sarandë waterfront. Strongest nightlife scene on the Riviera.
Day 5
Ksamil Islands + Blue Eye spring
Finest sand and clearest water on the coast. Swim to offshore islands. Afternoon: The Blue Eye (Syri i Kaltër) — surreal natural spring 20 min inland.
Day 6
Gjirokastër day trip or slow beach
Option A: 2h drive to Gjirokastër — UNESCO Ottoman stone city with castle. Option B: different beach, no agenda.
Day 7
Drive north, fly home
~3.5h drive to Tirana. Drop car at airport. Return flight to EIN (TIA departs 06:15 or 09:45).
⚠ July is peak — book flights and guesthouse now. Car rental sells out in July. The resort version (Drymades Inn Resort) pushes €750+/pp due to room prices.

Azores — São Miguel + Pico
Top pick

Nothing else in Europe like it — volcanic spa, whale watching, multi-island

NatureAdventureRemoteBeach
✈ EIN → LIS → PDL · 1 stop
€ ~€900–1000/pp
🏨 Octant Furnas + guesthouses
🚗 2× rental cars (separate islands)
Budget use€900–1000 / €1000
EIN → LIS (Transavia, ~3h) → PDL (Azores Airlines, ~2.5h)
Return ~€300–450/pp · Inter-island PDL→PIX: ~40min, €50–90/pp one way
Day 1
Arrive Ponta Delgada
Fly EIN→LIS→PDL. Pick up car. Check in Nine DOTS boutique (~€100–140/night). Explore Ponta Delgada waterfront.
Day 2
Sete Cidades crater lakes
45min west. Twin volcanic crater lakes — one of Portugal's 7 natural wonders. Rim hike 2–3h. Ferraria ocean thermal pools at sunset.
Days 3–4
Furnas Valley — Octant Furnas
Boiling mud pools, fumaroles, Terra Nostra gardens. Lunch: cozido stew cooked underground. 2 nights at Octant Furnas: thermal pools, spa, À TERRA restaurant.
Day 5
Nordeste coast
Dramatic sea cliffs, hydrangea roads, almost no tourists. Swim at Praia do Marreiros.
Day 6
Whale watching + Ribeira Quente
Boat from Ponta Delgada (~€55/pp). September: sperm whales + dolphins. Thermal springs in the sea at Ribeira Quente.
Day 7
Lagoa do Fogo hike
Crater lake in the island's centre (1.5h trail). Drive north: Santa Bárbara Eco-Beach bar for sundowners.
Days 8–9
Ponta Delgada + pineapple plantation
City exploration, covered market. Visit a pineapple greenhouse plantation (unique to Azores). Return car.
Day 10
Fly to Pico Island
Short flight PDL→PIX. New rental car. Check in Sea U Pico farmhouse (~€90–130/night). Walk UNESCO lava wine terraces at sunset.
Day 11
Mount Pico or lava pools
Option A: Summit Portugal's highest point, 2351m (7–8h, guide required). Option B: Lava rock pools, Whale Museum.
Day 12
Faial day trip by ferry
Ferry Madalena→Horta (20min). Horta marina murals, Capelinhos volcano (1957 eruption landscape). Return evening.
Day 13
Fly home via Lisbon
PIX or PDL→LIS→EIN. Return car at Pico airport. Evening arrival Eindhoven.
To stay within €1000/pp: swap Octant Furnas for Terra Nostra Garden Hotel next door (~€120/night), saving ~€150/pp. Book both car rentals and accommodation well in advance — Azorean accommodation is limited.

Lanzarote, Canary Islands

Volcanic island with lunar landscapes, boutique stays, and September's best beach weather

BeachNatureAdventureCulture
★ Best in September — crowds gone, sea warmest, prices lower
✈ EIN → ACE direct · 4h 20min
€ ~€580–720/pp
🏨 Boutique or rural finca
🚗 Rental car recommended
Budget use€580–720 / €1000
EIN → ACE (Lanzarote) — Transavia · direct
3× per week (Mon / Wed / Sat) · September return ~€120–190/pp · 4h 20min
Why September: sea at 22–23°C (warmest of the year), 26–28°C air, no school crowds, ~20% cheaper than July
Days 1–2
Arrecife + Puerto del Carmen base
Arrive, pick up rental car. Check in boutique or finca. Stroll Arrecife harbour promenade. Dinner at Puerto del Carmen old harbour (local fish restaurants, not tourist strip).
Day 3
Timanfaya National Park
The volcanic highlight of the island — lunar lava fields, geothermal geysers. Lunch at El Diablo restaurant where food is grilled on volcanic heat. Camel ride on the lava. Unmissable.
Day 4
La Geria wine valley + Teguise
Drive through the La Geria region: vines grown in volcanic ash hollows protected by low stone walls — unique viticultural landscape. Wine tasting at a local bodega. Afternoon: Teguise historic town (former capital).
Day 5
César Manrique trail
Jameos del Agua (lava cave converted to concert hall by architect Manrique — one of the most surreal spaces in Europe). Cueva de los Verdes (volcanic lava tube). Mirador del Río (clifftop viewpoint to La Graciosa island).
Day 6
La Graciosa island day trip
Ferry from Órzola (45min, ~€25/pp return). Tiny protected island with white sand beaches, no tarmac roads, almost no cars. Snorkel at Las Conchas beach — one of the best in the Canaries.
Day 7
Famara Beach + surf
Wild Atlantic north coast beach, dramatic cliff backdrop. Popular with surfers. Lesson or just watch. Lunch at one of the excellent fish restaurants overlooking the beach.
Day 8
Playa Blanca + ferry to Fuerteventura (optional)
Playa Blanca: Lanzarote's best beach resort, warm and sheltered. Optional: ferry to Corralejo, Fuerteventura (35min, ~€30/pp) for a day of massive sand dunes — worth it for the landscape alone.
Days 9–10
Slow down + fly home
Final beach day or snorkelling at Puerto del Carmen. Evening departure or morning flight home on the Mon/Wed/Sat schedule.
Why not July? July is peak season with stronger winds (good for kitesurfers, annoying at the beach), higher prices, and more package-holiday tourists. September delivers the same weather with 20% lower costs and a noticeably calmer atmosphere. This trip also leaves €280–420/pp unspent — plenty of room to upgrade accommodation or add activities.
Accommodation pick: avoid the big all-inclusive complexes entirely. Aim for Finca Malvasia (rural wine estate, ~€100–130/night), Lani's Suites de Luxe (adults-only boutique beachfront, ~€140–180/night), or Palacio Ico (9-room historic 1690 building in Teguise, ~€90–120/night).

Days 1–2
Athens
Acropolis + museum early morning. Monastiraki flea market. Psiri neighbourhood for dinner and nightlife. Pick up car day 2.
Days 3–4
Nafplio
Most beautiful small city in Greece. Venetian old town, harbour, Palamidi fortress. Day trips: Mycenae and Epidaurus ancient theatre.
Days 5–7
Mani Peninsula
Medieval tower villages, dramatic limestone landscape. Stay in converted tower house. Diros caves (underground lake by boat).
Days 8–10
Mystras + Sparta
Mystras: abandoned Byzantine city on the mountain — extraordinary and barely visited. Better than Pompeii for atmosphere.
Days 11–13
Olympia + north coast to ATH
Ancient Olympia. Drive north Peloponnese coast. Corinth canal. Return to Athens. Fly home.
The Mani is almost entirely unvisited by tourists despite being extraordinary. September: sea still 26°C, temperatures pleasant, no crowds.

Madeira

Year-round volcanic island — levada hikes, lush landscape, reliable sunshine

NatureAdventureRemote

🏨 Quinta manor house in the hills
🚗 Car recommended
Budget use€680–780 / €1000
EIN → FNC (Funchal, Madeira) — Transavia · direct
September return ~€100–160/pp
Days 1–3
Funchal
Old town market, cable car to Monte, Blandy's wine lodge Madeira tasting. One of the best small city arrivals in the Atlantic.
Days 4–6
Levada walks
Levadas: ancient irrigation canals with flat cliff-edge walking paths, unique to Madeira. Best: Levada das 25 Fontes and PR1 to Pico do Areeiro.
Days 7–8
North coast
Porto Moniz natural lava rock swimming pools. São Vicente caves. Greener and moodier than the south coast.
Days 9–10
Pico do Arieiro sunrise
Drive up at 4am for sunrise above the clouds at 1818m. Santana A-frame village houses. Machico bay for swimming.
Days 11–13
Ponta do Sol + fly home
Sunniest village in Madeira. Remote Valley trail. Final dinners in Funchal old town.
vs. Azores: Madeira is lusher, warmer, more reliable sunshine. Azores is more dramatic and unusual. For guaranteed sun + hiking, Madeira is the safer choice.
